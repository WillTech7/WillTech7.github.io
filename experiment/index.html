🏳️‍🌈 2022 Pride Month Fundraiser 🏳️‍🌈
I teamed up with an amazing group of game developers for pride month 2022 to raise money for an LGBTQ+ charity - the Albert Kennedy Trust. This is an incredible charity that supports homeless LGBTQ+ young people by providing them safe accommodation, mentoring and more.

My fitness challenge: Walk the distance from London to Brighton (LGBTQ+ capital of the UK) throughout June.

Thank you so much to everyone who donated, as a team we raised an incredible £5,300!! I also reached my distance goal half way through the month so decided to double it and managed to reach 100 miles by the end of June!
