Creations / SenseNet

The M5 AirQ SenseNet festures the advanced M5 AirQ hardware development kit with custom programming, hardware integration, and a web interface. Data collection on an external server saves long-term records of desired air metrics. These devices can be put in place for short or long-term air metric test periods.
