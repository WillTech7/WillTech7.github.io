Robotic Prosthetics

Creations / MechArm

Iterative robotic prosthetics

Over the course of two years, robotic prosthetic development was funded by the NASA Space Grant Fellowship. Several versions of the prosthetic were created with 3D printed parts and custom circuit boards. The arms and hand designs were used in graduate level research of patient brain activity during the operation of these prosthetics.
